About Us

Company Overview

Founded in 1995, Inter-Rig Supply & Engineering Pte Ltd has
grown into one of Singapore's leading metal fabrication
companies, delivering custom solutions in stainless steel, mild
steel, and aluminum. Our mission is to combine craftsmanship
with cutting-edge technology to build durable, high-
performance metal structures.

We Specialize In

METAL WELDING

Inter-Rig Supply and Engineering specialises in precision metal wielding for Singapore's
industrial, marine, and construction sectors. We specialize in structural steelwork, rig
components, and heavy-duty assemblies, combining CNC technology with certified welding
for unmatched durability and compliance. Businesses trust us for fast turnaround,
engineering expertise, and steel solutions built to last.
